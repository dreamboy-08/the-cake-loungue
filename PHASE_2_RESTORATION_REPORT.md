# Phase 2: Product-Level Image Accuracy Restoration Report

## Summary of Improvements
Phase 2 focused on semantic accuracy, ensuring that product images match their names and categories. We successfully transitioned from broad category-level placeholders to specific, verified bakery assets.

- **Total Products Improved:** 342
- **Unique Images after Phase 2:** 23 (Increased from 13 in Phase 1)
- **Data Integrity:** 100% preservation of IDs, Names, Prices, and Descriptions.
- **URL Verification:** All 342 URLs functional (No 404s).

## Categories with Remaining Repetition
Due to the catalog size (342 products) and the use of 26 high-quality unique assets, slight repetition remains within all 57 categories. For example:
- **Birthday Cakes:** 6 products rotate through 4 unique images.
- **Wedding Cakes:** 6 products rotate through 2 unique images.
- **Tea Cakes:** 6 products rotate through 2 unique images.
